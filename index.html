Our Materials

Built for a better planet

These are the core sustainable material lines we currently offer. Rinco works closely with leading fabric mills to continuously explore the frontier of eco-friendly materials — new options are always in development.

01

RPET

Sherpa · Canvas · Neoprene · Mesh · Oxford · Satin · Velvet · Corduroy · Terry

Recycled Polyethylene Terephthalate — giving plastic bottles a second life as beautiful, durable bags. An increasing number of corporations and retailers choose RPET over non-recycled synthetic fiber to reduce environmental impact.

02

Recycled Cotton

Pouches · Totes · Drawstring bags · Cosmetic bags

Transforming post-consumer textile waste into refined accessories. Recycled cotton consumes significantly less water than conventional cotton, while giving used fabrics a new and purposeful life.

03

FSC Paper Straw

Woven pouches · Tote bags · Cosmetic cases

Artisan-woven bags crafted from paper straw sourced from FSC-certified forests, ensuring responsible forest management. Each piece carries an organic, natural texture that is both tactile and earth-conscious.

More sustainable innovations in progress · Contact us to discuss custom material sourcing
